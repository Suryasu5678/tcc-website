Executive Health Physical

A Smarter Approach to Health

Our Executive Health Physical is designed for individuals who want a deeper understanding of their health – beyond the basics. It’s a comprehensive, one-day experience that combines a thorough workup with advanced testing and a thoughtful, personalized care plan.

This isn’t just a physical; it’s a proactive investment in your long-term health and wellness. We help you:

  • Detect issues early, before symptoms appear
  • Understand your unique risk profile
  • Create a plan to stay healthy, active, and well

What’s Included?

Heart & Vascular Health

  •  Cardiopulmonary fitness evaluation
  •   VO2 max and stress testing
  •  Carotid artery screening
  •  Abdominal aortic aneurysm screening
  •  Arterial and venous circulation screening
Lifestyle & Risk Factor Assessment
  •   Review of personal and family health history
  •  Evaluation of cardiovascular risk factors
  •  Guidance on fitness, nutrition, and stress management
Advanced Imaging & Testing (Optional)
  •  Total body MRI (to detect silent conditions early)
  •  Galleri® testing (to detect the “fingerprint” of many cancers before symptoms and beyond traditional screening)

Cardiology

What We Treat

From common concerns to complex heart issues, Twin Cities Cardiology is here to care for you. Some of the conditions we commonly treat include:

  • High blood pressure (hypertension)
  • Coronary artery disease (CAD)
  • Heart failure
  • Post-heart attack care
  • Heart disease in women
  • Prevention and risk assessment
  • High cholesterol
  • Chest pain or angina
  • Heart valve disease
  • Arrhythmias (like atrial fibrillation)
  • Congenital heart conditions
  • Cardiomyopathy

How We Help

At Twin Cities Cardiology, we use a combination of advanced cardiac imaging, electrodiagnostic studies, and AI-powered heart monitoring tools to understand the structure and function of your heart. Whether you're experiencing symptoms or simply seeking clarity, our goal is to detect issues early, guide you toward the right level of care, personalize your treatment, and provide ongoing support to care for your heart.

  • Echocardiogram (Transthoracic & Transesophageal)
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Stress Testing (Exercise)
  • Heart Monitoring
Cardiac Evaluations

We coordinate referral for specialized interventions, including surgery. Post-care follow-up is always part of the plan – because healing doesn’t stop at the hospital door.

  • Coronary angiography and stents
  • Pacemakers and cardiac ablations
  • CT coronary angiogram
  • MitraClip
  • Left atrial appendage closure (Watchman)
  • Cardiac MRI
  • Valve surgery
  • Transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR)
  • PFO closure

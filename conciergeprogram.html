Concierge Program

Elite Care. On Your Terms.

Experience the highest level of personalized care with our white glove service – because your time, health, and peace of mind are priceless. Our concierge program, with annual membership, offers unparalleled, physician-led health management that is discreet, proactive, and tailored to your life.

What’s Included?

Priority Access
  • Same-day or next-day scheduling
  • 24/7 communication with your physician via phone, text, or email
Comprehensive Health Management
  • Health and wellness, longevity planning, personalized prevention
  • Ongoing support for cardiology, vascular, and preventive needs
  • Genetic and biomarker testing, with personalized guidance whenever you need it
Lifestyle Integration
  • Collaboration with nutritionists, trainers, mental health experts, and performance coaches
  • Custom wellness plans that align with travel, business, and family life
Luxury-Level Experience
  • A calm, private, and welcoming environment
  • Thoughtful, unhurried care that puts you at the center
  • A proactive approach to your longevity

Who It’s For?

  • Convenience and flexibility
  • A uniquely personal relationship with their doctor and care team
  • Proactive approach to long and healthy living
  • Peace of mind knowing expert care is always within reach

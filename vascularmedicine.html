vascular Medicine

What We Treat

Your vascular health is important to us. We understand how issues with your veins and arteries can affect your daily life. That’s why we offer comprehensive care for a range of vascular conditions. Common vascular conditions we treat:

  • Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD)
  • Carotid Artery Disease
  • Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm
  • Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT)
  • Chronic Venous Insufficiency
  • Varicose and Spider Veins
  • Venous Ulcers
  • Lymphedema
  • Blood Clotting Disorders (Thrombophilia)
  • Raynaud’s Disease
  • Pelvic Congestion Syndrome
  • Post-thrombotic Syndrome

How We Help

At Twin Cities Cardiology, we focus on early detection, prevention, and minimally invasive care. Whether you’re dealing with a chronic condition or seeking relief from symptoms like leg pain, swelling, or visible veins, we’re here to help. We use advanced imaging, minimally invasive procedures, and personalized care to diagnose and treat vascular conditions. Twin Cities Cardiology is here to help you get back to your active lifestyle.
